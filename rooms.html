Type of Suites

All suites have been built to be spacious, airy, comfortable and picturesque, with ample bathrooms and modern amenities. Colors can be rich and vibrant or pastel and relaxing. All suites have been redecorated with local artwork and top of the line new full and king size mattresses.

Interior Suites (3 Total). These suites have no view, although they do have a nice sitting area and private outdoor patios, which make them our least expensive suites.

Pool View and Garden View Suites (11 Total). These suites have partial view to the hotel grounds. Three (3) of the pool view suites are on ground floor level, being accessible to all hotel and restaurant common areas.

Lake View Suites (5 Total). These suites have full breathtaking lake view, a nice sitting area and private terraces. As well as a common roof garden with mountain view.

Garden Villas (4 Total). One and two Bedroom Garden Villas which are available upon request. These villas come with living room, dinning area and small kitchenettes. Ideal for long term clients, which minimum stay is 1 month.

We offer two dining areas to our guests both with commanding views of the lake. One is a beautiful patio protected from the sun by a giant rubber tree amidst our lush tropical garden. We also have an elegant indoor dining area. Our cuisine is a mix of Continental and Mexican fare.

Features include 24 hour Reception with bilingual staff; indoor/outdoor dining; conference/meeting rooms; Cable TV with 14 English language channels.  For more information check our "Features" Page.

Located across from the hotel, we also have one and two bedroom furnished Garden Villas with full kitchens. Each bedroom has its own bathroom, 2 color TVs with 14 English channels.

To Reserve a room aside we require a major credit card number. You can email it in three parts and send it in three different emails or you can phone it in to us. If you are dialing from the U.S. or Canada.

Additional information

Smoking policy: This property has smoking and non-smoking rooms. Credit cards accepted: American Express, MasterCard, Visa. Restaurant closed: Open from 7:00am to 11:00pm.
