VIDEO OF HOTEL NUEVA POSADA, AJIJIC AND CHAPALA

Rooms are tastefully decorated with colonial furniture and original watercolors created by local artists. Most of the Suites have breath-taking lake views and the modern features of a grand hotel.

We offer two dining areas to our guests both with commanding views of the lake. One is a beautiful patio protected from the sun by a giant rubber tree amidst our lush tropical garden. We also have an elegant indoor dining area. Our cuisine is a mix of Continental and Mexican fare.

Features include 24 hour Reception with bilingual staff; indoor/outdoor dining; conference/meeting rooms; Cable TV with 14 English language channels.  For more information check our "Features" Page.

Located across from the hotel, we also have one and two bedroom furnished Garden Villas with full kitchens. Each bedroom has its own bathroom, 2 color TV’s with 14 English channels.

To Reserve a room aside we require a major credit card number. You can email it in three parts and send it in three different emails or you can phone it in to us. If you are dialing from the U.S. or Canada.

Additional information

Smoking policy: This property has smoking and non-smoking rooms. Credit cards accepted: American Express, MasterCard, Visa. Restaurant closed: Open from 7:00am to 11:00pm.
